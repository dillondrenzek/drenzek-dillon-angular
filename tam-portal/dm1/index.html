Visiting an Old Friend

I heard it said recently that "This Too Shall Pass." That really struck a chord with me, so I decided I'd take that and run with it. The idea with this picture is that on the left, the kid does the graffiti early in his life and then comes back sometime later to see that his graffiti has eroded.

Me in So Many Words

I just couldn't stop thinking about skiing so I made this composition. Its a collage of quite a few different things. That is me in the upper right, my skis can be seen in the orange/brown region, my name is in the lower right. And, I like to think of myself as a lone wolf (in this case, tree), particularly when I ski. Nothing better than getting and reconnecting with the mountains.
